About Libertarianism

Libertarianism is a political philosophy that upholds liberty as a core principle. Libertarians seek to maximize autonomy and political freedom, emphasizing free markets, civil liberties, and non-interventionism.

This project is dedicated to promoting libertarian ideas and values.

Key Principles

  • Free Markets
  • Limited Government
